REMOTE SHOOTING

A new way to create together from anywhere in the world

What is Remote Shooting?

Remote shooting is a new way of shooting, allowing photographers from anywhere in the world to photograph me from my location through modern technology. I set up my camera on a tripod connected to my laptop with Canon EOS Utility, which you control remotely via TeamViewer. We communicate through Skype or another voice call while you control the camera settings live.

What does the photographer need?

  • Laptop or computer with stable internet connection
  • TeamViewer software installed
  • Basic knowledge or practice session recommended

Control over the camera

EOS Utility lets you control focus points, manual settings, shutter and preview images in real time. You can monitor histogram and review previews instantly.
