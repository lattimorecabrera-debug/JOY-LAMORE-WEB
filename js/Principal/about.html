ABOUT ME

It all started when I was a little girl and loved to be in front of the camera. What began as curiosity slowly transformed into purpose.

In 2017, I decided to take it seriously. Since then, I have worked with incredible photographers, travelled across countries, and explored different ways of expressing emotion through the body.

Now I am a full professional model from Madrid, Spain. I deeply like being in front of the camera , it makes me free and alive. I love art and I love to be part of it. I am outgoing, hardworking and resourceful.I consider myself versatile and creative. I am chatty, I have a good humor and I really like to laught and have fun. Life is complicated but beautiful so, always smile!

As a model, I am available for travel world wide and to be hired for professional and amateur photographers, posing for brands, artists, videographers, make-up artists, hairstylists and designers for printed work, advertising, exhibitions, publications, commercials and portfolio purposes as well as for workshops, group shoots or modeling sharing. I am very experience in workshops with big groups as well as with small groups or private workshops. I've been publishing in many online and printed magazines as Solstice Magazine, Volo magazine, Crush magazine, RHK magazine, Penthouse Germany, Maxim Italy and I am miss November 2019 in Playboy magazine Spain.

I have a wide range of expressions and poses and I always work to be better and get new ones. I mostly get inspired about life experiences, feelings , music and cinema.I like and I can work in every single style of photography even if I mostly do nude.

During the shoot I try to connect with myself and with the photographer's ideas to catch the essence of the shot and create a beautiful work.

I am able to do my own makeup and hairstyle. I can pose with or without direction. I am up to hike, climb, get wet, swimn, dive and go into cold andhot, in order to get "The Shot", always is everything well organized and I do not fear for my own safety.

I did rhythmic gymnastics and I can bend, stretch and contort myself into many different and unique shapes and position.

I am able to do suspended shibari as well as normal shibari.I am open to discuss and learn any specific abilities you need for the shoot you want to make.

If you have any questions please don't hesitate to contact me!
