REMOTE SHOOTS

Imagine you are a photographer who’d love to have a shooting with me but we are miles away. Is there any solution? YES! REMOTE SHOOTING can easily sort this out

We do everything over the internet. We discuss the location, my looks and style beforehand. Remote shooting is a new way of shooting, allowing photographers from virtually anywhere in the world to photograph me from my location through the wonders of modern technology. This method allows for quality images to be taken and for photographers to have control over the settings of the camera, unlike zoom/facetime shoots. In short: I set up my camera on a tripod and connect it to my laptop with Canon EOS utility software, which you remotely control on your laptop via TeamViewer. We then use Skype or another app to make a voice call to communicate. There is an assistant that would help moving the camera, etc.
